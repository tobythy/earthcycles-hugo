It’s the bike for everyone – an affordable combination of Yuba’s best bikes!

Meet the ultimate urban people mover – from grocery-getting to dominating the school drop off, the Kombi is the right tool for any job. This cargo bike is affordable, portable and storable. Its name is a nod to the adventurer and the explorer in all of us. This is your gateway to two-wheeled freedom, shared adventures and new discoveries!

Yuba took the best parts of their most popular models and kombined them (see what we did there?) to create a cargo bike for everybody. For the urban dweller, a shorter wheelbase for the bus rack and upright storage solutions for families that live in apartments. For the parents, we included disc brakes for safety, a low step over for easy sharing and a rear rack spacious enough for three kids and all their gear for a day on the go.

Nimble, snappy handling under load was the top priority because let’s face it, the city can be chaotic. With clever design and complementary components, you can carry your cargo with comfort and confidence, from the family dog to a Sunday market find.

Components

  • 9 Speed Shimano Altus Shadow drivetrain, 11-40 gear range
  • Tektro Aries Mechanical Disc Brakes
  • Kenda Puncture Resistant tires with K-Shield

Details

  • Comfortably carries 2-3 kids
  • Compatible with Yepp Nexxt child seat
  • Compatible with many Yuba Add-Ons

What’s included

  • Kickstand
  • Front Fender + Rear Wheelskirt
  • Puncture-resistant tires
  • Deflopilator
  • Bell
  • 2-year Limited Warranty
