The Ultimate Urban People Mover

This electric cargo bike has both brains and brawn. The intelligent and powerful pedal assist system masterfully offers just the right amount of assist for a natural feel under your feet. You’ll actually enjoy running all those pesky errands and seeking out those hills you used to avoid. Go ahead and load it up – the Electric Boda Boda is designed to haul anything life throws at you while keeping that light, nimble steering for a super smooth ride, every time. Confidently carry your kids, some shopping or a stack of wood without breaking a sweat.

Features

  • e6000 Shimano Steps electric kit with 3 levels of assist + walk mode
  • Easy to read Integrated Cycling Computer with speed, distance, trip time, clock, gear, estimated range, battery indication.
  • Choose from a Step-Thru or Step-Over frame
  • Always charged front and rear LED lights
  • Chain guard, Fenders and Wheelskirts included
  • Double Stand included
  • Hauls up to 100kg of cargo PLUS the rider
  • 2 year warranty on Yuba Frame and 2 year warranty on the Shimano STEPS electric system
  • Weighs in at just 27kg

Boda Boda Specifications

  • Frame material: Aluminum
  • Fork: 1.5” Chromoly
  • Drivetrain: 9 Speed Shimano Altus Shadow
  • Brakes: Tektro Vela Hydraulic Disc Brakes
  • Cockpit: Adjustable stem

Shimano STEPS Specifications

  • e6000 Shimano STEPS motor Delivers an output torque of 50Nm for ultimate performance
  • Weatherproof and waterproof cases for motor and battery
  • High-quality Li-Ion battery, fully certified 418 Wh with quick charging (EC-E6000): 80% in 2 hours, from 0 to 100% in 4 hours
  • Battery Range is 20 – 93 miles per charge (dependant on load and terrain)
