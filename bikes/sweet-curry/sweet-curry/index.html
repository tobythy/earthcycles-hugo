The Sweet Curry is a pedal powered version of its electrified brother the Spicy Curry. Sure to be an instant crowd favorite, the Sweet Curry boasts a super strong but light aluminium frame of oversized tubing, 20” rear wheel and extra long rear deck for maximum hauling capacity, ready to haul up to 130kg.

Compatible with all the best addons, the Sweet Curry is pretty much the ultimate pedal powered family cargo bike.

The Sweet Curry cargo bike comes equipped with tough Schwalbe Big Apple Plus tires and VP 1.5” Conventional Threadless headset. Also included are fenders, rear skirt guards and a beautiful bright orange paint job. One size frame fits most riders.

Features

  • A big wheel and a little wheel!
  • Practical with the Yuba Add-On cargo system
  • Carries long, bulky and tall cargo
  • Low-rider cargo rack with 20 inch rear wheel for increased stability and load safety
  • Sturdy aluminium frame
  • One size frames fits most riders 5′ to 6’5” = 1.5m to 2m

SPECS

  • Frame: Sweet Curry 6061 Aluminum w/Disc Tabs
  • Fork: Sweet Curry Cr-Mo 1.5” Threadless w/Disc Tabs
  • Sideloaders: Sold Separately

COCKPIT

  • Headset: Neco 1.5” Conventional Threadless
  • Stem: Promax 1.5” Adjustable Stem 100mm
  • Handlebars: Intek 31.8mm City
  • Handlebar Grips: Yuba Ergo Grip w/Clamp
  • Saddle: Velo Comfort Saddle
  • Seatpost: 30.9mm Aluminum 500mm

BRAKE KIT

  • Levers: Avid Mechanical
  • Brake: Avid BB7 Mechanical
  • Rotor: Avid G2 (180mm Front | 180mm Rear)

TRANSMISSION

  • Front Derailleur: Shimano Claris FD-2400
  • Rear Derailleur: Shimano Claris RD-2400
  • Shift Levers: Shimano Claris SL-2400 2×8-Speed Trigger Shifter
  • Cassette: Shimano 11-32T 8-Speed |
  • Chain: KMC 8-Speed
  • Crankset: Samox 170mm 50-34
  • Pedals: VP-501 Alloy

WHEELS

  • Rim: Shining DB-32 MTB Double Wall (36H Front | 48H Rear)
  • Hubs: Chosen Cr-Mo QR (36H Front| 48H Rear)
  • Tyres: Schwalbe Big Apple Plus (26” x 2.15” Front | 26” x 2.15” Rear)

EXTRAS & ADD-ONS

  • Included: Bell, Fenders, Sturdy Side Stand
  • Optional: 2-Go bags, Bread Basket, Carry-Ons, Child Seat, Hold-Ons, Mini Monkey Bars, MultiUtility Deck, Ring, Side Boards, * Soft Spot, Stand Alone
