Eight and a half feet of cargo hauling happiness

From kegs to kids, this cargo bike can haul it all, no sweat required. Equipped with a powerful and efficient Bosch motor and intentionally designed to be the smoothest steering front loader ever, the Electric Supermarche is destined to deliver.

With features like infinite gear ratios, easy to mount step-through frame and adjustable cockpit, hauling up to 135kgs of kiddos or cargo has never felt so natural. Parents will love the safety extras like always charged LED lights and ultra-responsive hydraulic disc brakes. With plenty of room for innovation, the Electric Supermarché is compatible with lots of great Add-Ons to customize your ride.

Say goodbye to the minivan in the driveway and say hello to the cargo bike lifestyle. With two options: ‘No front box’ is the bare frame, so you can build your own creation to cargo your cargo. The ‘With Front Box’ option has the 4 piece interlocking Bamboo ply front box included. Perfect for carrying packages, pets or precious little ones. You can also purchase the accessory seating option for the kids.

The Electric Supermarché is outfitted with the powerful and whip-smart Bosch Performance Line CX Motor and a long-lasting PowerPack 400 battery (Optional 500 available). This system offers speed and stamina with smooth transitions. The whole system is lightweight and quiet so you get that natural bike riding feel, even with a little help.

Bosch Features:

  • 4 Levels of Pedal Assist
  • PowerPack 400 36V battery
  • Battery range up to 55 miles per charge
  • Max Torque – 75 Nm
  • Sleek Purion display

Supermarché Features:

  • Light aluminium frame with strong Chromoly fork
  • Ultra smooth Dual Cable Steering
  • Integrated Yepp mounting bracket in rear rack
  • Adjustable cockpit and seat post for easy sharing
  • Magura Hydraulic Disc Brakes
  • Enviolo internally geared hub

Included:

  • Always charged LED front and rear lights
  • Full coverage fenders, chain guard and front wheelskirt
  • Double-sided kickstand
